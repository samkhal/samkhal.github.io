Building on research done by Rob Platt, I worked on identification of object handles and automatic grasping of objects. The robot CARL was equipped with a 3-fingered 6-DOF JACO arm, which I used for the grasping tasks. Object handles were identified by processing pointcloud data from a Kinect mounted on CARL.
